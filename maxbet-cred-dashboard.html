Get ahead of Romania's proposed deposit cap

Romania's regulator has been pushing for ~2 years to limit player activity to roughly 10% of monthly income — on €3,000/mo, that's a €300 ceiling. It may not pass, but if it does, knowing affordability in advance lets Maxbet front-run it: protect compliant high-value play, flag at-risk spenders for affordability review, and keep CRM firepower on genuine VIPs instead of guessing.
